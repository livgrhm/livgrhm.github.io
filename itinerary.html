Delhi

Day 1 & 2.
Support Planeterra's New Delhi Streetkids Project on an included walk led by a former street kid before an afternoon orientation walk in Old Delhi. Overnight train to Bikaner.

Sept 07

Bikaner

Day 3 & 4.
Camel safari and camp overnight in the desert under the stars. Orientation walk of the Bikaner market and the old city.

Sept 09

Jaisalmer

Day 5 & 6.
Orientation walk around Jaisalmer, then time to explore and check out its ancient yet still inhabited fort.

Sept 11

Jodhpur

Day 7.
Orientation walk around town, then opt to visit the Mehrangarh Fort.

Sept 13

Udaipur

Day 8 & 9.
Get to know the old city on an orientation walk. Opt to visit the City Palace, or take in an evening dance performance at Bagore Ki Haveli.

Sept 15

Pushkar

Day 10.
Upon arrival in Pushkar, explore the city on an orientation walk. Opt to tour the world's only temple to the Hindu god of creation, Brahma, scour the markets for Indian wares, or watch the sunrise over this sacred city after a walk up to Savitri Temple.

Sept 17

Tordi Sagar

Day 11.
Dive into rural life and visit a potter and other local areas of a village. Opt to take a jeep safari to the dunes for sunset, head for a hike in the hills or get a henna tattoo with the local women.

Sept 18

Jaipur

Day 12 & 13.
Before leaving Tordi Sagar, enjoy a local lunch prepared by traditional methods at a village hamlet, then continue to Jaipur. Enjoy an orientation walk around town and a guided tour of the Amber Fort. Take some free time to explore other sites around town.

Sept 19

Agra

Day 14 & 15.
Marvel at the Taj Mahal on a guided tour. Opt to explore the Agra Fort and Baby Taj.

Sept 21

Varanasi

Day 16, 17 & 18.
Cruise the Ganges River by boat at sunrise and sunset and enjoy a candle flower ceremony. Overnight train back to Delhi.

Sept 23

Delhi

Day 19 & 20. 2 Days in hotel perfect
